About the Artist

Junior Lopes is a contemporary plastic artist from Pará who has revolutionized the contemporary art scene. For 15 years, he has developed an innovative technique in graphic illustration with recycled textiles.

His work questions waste and reaffirms Amazonian creativity in global dialogue. Each piece: cutting, layering, stitching. No paint, no pigment. Fabric alone.

Denim carries histories. Textiles bring exuberance. Together, they converse about memory, consumption and resistance in the context of contemporary mass culture.
